Open the site you want to check, then click Scan this site. The page reloads with cookies cleared so the scan reflects a first visit with no consent given.
Tests whether tracking actually stops once consent is withdrawn (no reload — your accepted consent state is preserved throughout):
  1. Click Start test, then accept consent and browse the page normally.
  2. Use the site's own controls to withdraw consent (reject all / disable categories / do not sell).
  3. Click Mark withdrawal immediately after. Results appear ~15s later.
